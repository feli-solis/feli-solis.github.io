Web application for JUARDE

The white and yellow pages of San Miguel de Allende, known as JUARDE (Who Are They?) . A telephone directory which has become part of this unique, multicultural community of nationals and foreigners. With 56 years in the market, it has positioned itself as the product with the longest trajectory.

Challenge
